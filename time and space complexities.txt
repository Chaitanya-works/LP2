time and space complexities:

DFS: 
time: O(V+E)
space: O(V)

BFS:
time: O(V+E)
space: O(V)

A-star: best search
time: O(b^d)
space: O(b^d)
b: branching(avg. no. of successor per state)
d:maximum depth of search

Prims:
time: O(V^2)
space: O(V^)
using adjacency matrix

time: O((V+E)logV)
using adjacency list

Kruskal's
time: O(ElogE)
space: O(V+E)

N-queens backtracking:
time: worst: O(N!)

N-queens branch and bound
O(N^2)
